Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Push-Up Challenge.

What is the Push-Up Challenge?

The Push-Up Challenge is C&C Inc's signature discount: complete 20 push-ups on the spot while we're hooking up your vehicle, and we'll take $20 off your towing bill. It's our way of making a stressful roadside situation a little lighter.

Can anyone do the Push-Up Challenge?

Yes! The challenge is available to any customer who wants to try. Young or old, we've had all kinds of people give it a shot. It's completely optional—you'll still get great service even if push-ups aren't your thing.

Why does C&C Inc offer this?

Getting stranded is stressful. Owner Chucky started the Push-Up Challenge as a way to lighten the mood and have a little fun with customers. It's become a local favorite and something that makes C&C Inc different from every other towing company.

Where is the challenge available?

The Push-Up Challenge is available throughout our service area: Roscommon, Houghton Lake, Higgins Lake, Prudenville, Grayling, and all of Roscommon County.

What if I can't do 20 push-ups?

No problem at all! The challenge is just for fun. You'll still receive the same professional, friendly service we give every customer. And hey, maybe you'll come back next time ready to earn that $20!

Can I film my attempt?

Absolutely! With your permission, we love filming successful attempts to share on our page. Many customers have become local legends after their Push-Up Challenge videos went up. It's all in good fun!
